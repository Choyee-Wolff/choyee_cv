About me

Why I Chose Data

My passion for data began during a university project where I analysed survey data to understand the patterns behind student success. What started as a simple exercise in spreadsheets quickly turned into a fascination with uncovering hidden insights. I realised that data wasn’t just numbers. It told stories, revealed opportunities, and had the power to solve real-world problems.

What drives me the most in this field is the feeling of achievement when I solve a challenging problem. Whether it’s untangling a messy dataset, building a predictive model, or delivering a dashboard that changes the way people see their business, I thrive on the satisfaction of finding solutions that make a real impact. For me, working with data is more than a job. It’s a rewarding journey of discovery and problem-solving.

Hobbies

I enjoy engaging in DIY projects, which allow me to be creative and hands-on in building or improving things around me. Another activity I deeply enjoy is planning trip itineraries, where I get to research destinations, organise schedules, and craft memorable travel experiences. These hobbies keep me energised and constantly curious about the world around me.
